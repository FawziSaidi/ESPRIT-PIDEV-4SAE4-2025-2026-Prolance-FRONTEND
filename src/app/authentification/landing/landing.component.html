Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is Prolance really commission-free? expand_more

Yes. Freelancers keep 100% of their earnings. We monetize through optional premium features for recruiters, not by taking a cut from your hard work.

How fast do I get paid after a milestone? expand_more

Payments are released within 24 hours of milestone approval. Funds go directly to your linked bank account or PayPal.

How does Prolance verify jobs and clients? expand_more

Every job posting and client account goes through a manual review by our Trust & Safety team. We verify identities, company details, and payment methods before anything goes live.

Can I use Prolance for both freelancing and hiring? expand_more

Absolutely. A single Prolance account lets you switch between freelancer and recruiter modes at any time. Many of our power users do both.

What happens if there's a dispute? expand_more

Our built-in escrow and mediation system protects both parties. If a disagreement arises, our support team steps in to review the work and resolve the issue fairly.
